Expertise

I apply artificial intelligence to solve data related real-world problems.

Availability of data has made everything much easier and it is safe to say that our current world revolves a lot around data. Be that as it may, the data itself is useless until it has been interpreted, visualized and in some cases engineered. This is where I come in, applying machine learning, deep learning and data engineering to make the most of data.

  • Machine Learning

    I have applied machine learning to solve several real-world problems. I have used machine learning for predictive modelling, clustering, and optimization

  • Deep Learning

    I have experience with Artificial Neural Network (ANN) and Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) and I have built projects with both deep learning methods. I am also experienced with transfer learning.

  • Data Engineering

    Sometimes the dataset presented doesn't give all the information in plain sight calling for further manipulation of the data to get required information. I have on several occassions applied data engineering to make intuitive decisions and inference.

  • Database Management System

    I have vast experience with DBMS softwares like MySQL and SQLite and relational DBMS method like SQL.

  • Computer Vision

    Computer vision is how we AI engineers tell the computer what to 'see'. I have created several projects with computer vision using latest technologies.

  • Natural Language Processing

    Natural language processing is much more than text mining. I have created transformer projects that extract required information from texts, images and speech.

  • Physical Chemistry Research

    Prior to my venturing into AI and Data Science, I spent about four years studying the computational models of bentonite clay. I did molecular dynamic studies of the effect of Fe(III), monovalent interlayer cations and divalent interlayer cations on the swelling pressure of bentonite clay. Bentonite clay has widely accepted as the most suitable buffer/sealant material in underground repositories for long-term disposal of high level radioactive waste. I published innovative findings from my research in top scientific journals and presented them in top conferences.
